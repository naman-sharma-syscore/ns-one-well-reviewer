01 — Foundations

Type, color, surface

FT Made (display) is reserved for serene, editorial moments — page titles, hero numbers, certifications. Mazzard Soft M (body) carries everything else. Cyan is the system's signal: focus, primary CTA gradient, active state. Blue is utilitarian — buttons, links, panel strokes. Concept colors are only for content tagging.

Display sample
Health is a design decision.
FT Made 42px / 400 paired with Mazzard Soft M 16px / 400 in body. Display sets a calm, editorial tone; body keeps interfaces approachable.

02 — Buttons & Actions

CTAs and utility actions

Pill-shaped CTAs at 64px (XL) or 48px (LG) for hero moments. Utility buttons are 32px and rectangular. The cyan gradient is the brand "yes" — use it sparingly, once per view.
