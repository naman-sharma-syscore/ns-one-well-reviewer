Color System

The complete color story for One WELL. 13 brand scales (130 swatches), 11 concept colors, 4 rating colors, 4 certification tiers, 4 gradients, blur effects, and surface usage rules. All values verified against client/global.css.

01

Brand Scales — 13 × 10 shades

130 tokens
Every interface color descends from these 13 ladders. Each scale runs from 50 (lightest) to 900 (darkest). Use the role hint to know when to reach for which scale.
02

Concept Colors

11 tokens
Reserved exclusively for tagging WELL content concepts — never carry UI state. They appear on theme/strategy/requirement codes, breadcrumbs, and concept icons.
03

Rating Colors

4 tokens
Identify the four WELL rating tracks. Used on rating seals, scoring bars, and rating-specific badges.
04

Certification Tiers

4 tokens
The four WELL certification levels. Each tier is a metallic-feeling solid backed by its own 10-step scale (see Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum above) for full UI usage.
05

Gradients

4 tokens
Reserved for hero CTAs, performance dashboards, and section transitions. Use sparingly — at most one gradient per screen.
CTA Bright
--gradient-cta-bright
Hero CTAs on light surfaces. Cyan‑400 → Emerald‑400 → Light‑emerald‑200.
CTA Cyan
--gradient-cta-cyan
Default primary CTA. Bright gradient with cyan‑800 multiply for legibility.
Soft
--gradient-soft
Empty states, onboarding hero, marketing surfaces. Emerald → Plum → Blue.
Performance
--gradient-performance
Performance score bars. Green → cyan → plum → coral as score drops.
06

Blur Effects

Surface & Background Usage

When to reach for which surface. Don't invent new fills — every UI surface in One WELL maps to one of these tokens.
RoleTokenHexWhen to use
Page background--background#F9F9FAThe default page canvas. Holds elevated cards and panels.
Surface (card)--surface#FFFFFFCards, modals, dropdowns, primary content containers.
Side rails--surface-side#FAFEFFLeft/right navigation rails and supporting panels.
Overlay surface--surface-overlay#3E4049Tooltips and dark overlays. Always paired with white type.
Border (default)--border#DEDFE3Default 1px border on cards, inputs, tables, dividers.
Border (soft)--border-soft#EFF1F2Internal dividers within a card; secondary separators.
